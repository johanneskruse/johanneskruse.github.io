Our paper **ZoRRO: A Zero-Weight Personalized Recommender System for Scalable News Recommendation** is appearing at the ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval.

The work presents a scalable approach to personalized news recommendation developed with Ryotaro Shimizu, Kasper Lindskow, Jon Tofteskov, Michael Riis Andersen, Julian McAuley, and Jes Frellsen.
